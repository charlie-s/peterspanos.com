Peter Spanos

Peter Spanos CEO of Vetta Sports Clubs, has seven indoor sports facilities in the St. Louis, Missouri metropolitan area. With soccer as its base, Vetta Sports has experience in multiple sports, managing multiple locations and providing programming at several outdoor sites. Pete is a former Division I soccer player and earned his MBA prior to founding Vetta Sports. Driven by his passion to teach life skills through sports, instruction became the building block in the early years of Vetta. Adult and youth leagues round out the core offerings. Currently, Mr. Spanos spends time on management development and has been one of the lead instructors for US Indoor’s Certified Arena Operators management certification.

Vetta, named after Pete and John Spanos’ grandmother, has a long-term committed executive team with 300 FTE’s and nearly 2 million visits per year. The team has managed diverse sports such as tennis, racquetball, volleyball, basketball, and fitness centers.

Pete was the founding Board Chairman for America Scores - St. Louis, a not-for-profit urban soccer and writing after school program. He was also co-chairman of Growth Development and Construction of his church’s $8.5 million Family Life Center.
